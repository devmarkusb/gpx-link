Maps GPX gives you direct links from GPX POIs into Google Maps details. Lightweight viewer for GPX files, tracks with POIs.

<b>What you can do</b>
• Open one or many GPX files and selectively view them on a map
• Tap waypoint markers to open Google Maps with all the familiar richness of details
• Short title information is shown marker labels

<b>Who it is for</b>
Campers, hikers, cyclists, runners, road-trip planners and anyone who already has GPX exports and wants a fast map-first view on their phone.

<b>Recommendation</b>
Use in combination with GPX POI Enricher https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.cismypa.gpxpoienricher&hl=en-US
to automatically find interesting POIs along a route and save them along the track as GPX - to be opened here again.

<b>Privacy</b>
The app loads map tiles and scripts over the network (OpenStreetMap / CDNs) when you view the map. It may show third-party ads; you can remove ads permanently with a one-time in-app purchase from the project menu. No account required.

<b>Open source</b>
Built from the same GPX rendering core as the gpx-link project on GitHub — handy if you use the desktop or CLI tools too.
